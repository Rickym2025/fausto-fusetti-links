  • Competition: The market is saturated with events, and attracting the public requires creativity and innovation.
  • Logistics: Managing the safety, hospitality, and transport of thousands of people is a complex task that requires great organization.
  • Environmental sustainability: Festivals have a significant impact on the environment. Sustainable practices must be adopted to reduce the ecological footprint.
  • Human resource management: Finding and managing qualified personnel is essential for the success of the event.

In such a complex context, collaboration becomes a key element. Organizers must be able to create synergies with other operators in the sector, institutions, sponsors, and artists.
