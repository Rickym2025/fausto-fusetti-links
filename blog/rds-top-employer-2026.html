What Does It Mean to Be a Top Employer?

The Top Employer certification is not just a quality seal. It represents an in-depth and independent assessment of working conditions within a company. The Top Employers Institute analyzes various aspects, including:

  • Human Resources Strategy: How the company plans and manages its human capital.
  • Work Environment: The quality of the physical and relational environment in which employees operate.
  • Talent Acquisition: How the company attracts and selects the best talent on the market.
  • Training and Development: The professional growth opportunities offered to employees.
  • Well-being: The company's initiatives aimed at promoting the physical and mental well-being of employees.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: The company's commitment to creating a fair and inclusive work environment for all.

Obtaining the Top Employer certification means that RDS excels in all these areas, demonstrating a strong commitment to its employees and creating a positive and stimulating work environment.
