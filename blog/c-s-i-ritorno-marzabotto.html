The news of C.S.I.'s return came like a bolt from the blue, fueled by rumors and speculations that had been circulating among fans for some time. An Indelible Legacy

Beyond future projects, the return of C.S.I. represents an opportunity to rediscover and celebrate a musical and cultural legacy of inestimable value.
