The partnership between Radio Number One and Team Level Up MTA is not new. In past seasons, the broadcaster has already supported the team, bringing its energy and passion for engines to the paddock. The choice to focus on the Moto3 category is not accidental. This class represents the future of motorcycling, with young talents from all over the world ready to compete for the title.
